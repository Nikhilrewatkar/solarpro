Our Solar Installation Process

1. Site Assessment

Our team visits your location to evaluate the roof structure, shade, and space availability.

2. System Design

We create a custom solar design that suits your roof and maximizes energy production.

3. Approval & Permits

We handle all government approvals and paperwork for a hassle-free experience.

4. Installation

Our certified technicians install the panels, inverters, and electrical connections safely.

5. Activation & Handover

After final testing, your system is activated, and you begin your solar savings journey!
